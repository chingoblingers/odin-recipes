Pico de Gallo

Description

Have you ever wondered how to make your own pico de gallo? In a few easy steps i will show you how you can make delicious pico with your fresh Ingredients so you never need to go store bought again!

Ingredients

  • 5 tomato's
  • 5 onions
  • cilantro
  • lime juice
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. dice onions, cilantro and tomato's
  2. mix together all vegetables in a container
  3. add lime juice, salt, and pepper
  4. shake together and let sit for 5 min.
  5. enjoy
