Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken alfredo is a name that pleases the ears of many. Lean protein, creamy white sauce and delicious strands of pasta, what isn't there to love? Ill be showing you how you can make this great recipe and fill the stomachs of many for days to come.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of boneless skinless chicken breast
  • olive oil
  • Salt and Black Pepper
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Shredded parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the Fettuccine al dente
  2. Season chicken and bake on 350F in oven for 30 minutes. After ward cut into 1/2 thick slices
  3. Mix butter and seasonings in a pan pre simmered on medium heat until thickened. Afterward add cheese and stir until smooth
  4. Drain pasta and add to mixed sauce, Finally adding into serving bowls of your choice.
